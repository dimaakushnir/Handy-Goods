Kite backpacks

Kite backpacks have undergone technical testing in accordance with the European standards DIN 33419 / EN ISO 15537 at the German Institute of Health and Ergonomics (Institute für Gesundheit und Ergonomie) and received an honorary distinction “Ergonomic product”. Kite backpacks are customized for body and age features of elementary schoolchildren, with care for their comfort and proper healthy posture.

Model 501

Box shape is a perfect model for the first-graders. Owing to such shape and thickened frame this backpack does not get deformed and makes it easy for the child to pack things. Even after chaotic packing there will be order inside the backpack! Model 501 features patented orthopedic back Ergo Kids with “breathing” pads, which repeat the natural curve of the spine and train the child to keep a straight back. Model 501 is perfect for first-graders from 120 cm tall.
Model characteristics
Patented orthopedic back Ergo Kids
  • features supporting soft pads along the spine and on the lower back
  • repeats the natural curve of the spine
  • reduces pressure to the back
  • trains to keep the back straight and not to slouch
S-shaped ergonomic straps
  • ensure tight fit of the backpack
  • prevent discomfort for shoulders and neck
Removable chest strap
  • equally distributes weight across all sections of the spine
  • prevents pressure to the lower back
Dividers for books
  • firmly hold textbooks, help keep the gravity center in place
  • maintain child’s body balance during movement
  • keep the content of the backpack in order
Reinforced bottom
  • does not sag under weight of books
  • maintains backpack stability
  • easy to clean even with a moist tissue
Light reflecting details
  • protect the child on the road during hours of darkness
  • serve as an additional element of design

Model 531

Model 531 features patented orthopedic back Ergo Kids and a unique system to adjust the height of the back to the child’s height. This way the back of this backpack “grows” together with the child. This backpack will last through elementary school years and will help maintain healthy posture. Hard front panel will keep school supplies from being deformed and will not fail your child when riding down a snow hill.
