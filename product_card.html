Wonder Kite School SET_WK22-583S-1 includes 3 most important school items – backpack, pencil case and shoe bag. They designed in one single color palette and together make a perfect match with each other. This stylish capsule set combines premium quality, advanced functionality and trendy design.

Wonder Kite line has already become a favorite one for kids and their parents. The VIP 3-in-1 Set will save your time during school preparations and will give your child maximum of comfort.

1. School backpack Kite SET_WK22-583S-1 is a semi-hard-shaped model made of durable polyester. This orthopedic model provides comfort for the child and maintains the correct posture.

This model is approved by the German Institute of Health and Ergonomics IGR and it is developed considering the age of the children and their physical characteristics. It is recommended for kids aged 6-8 years old with a height of 115-130 cm. The backpack opens and closes in just one movement. The FIDLOCK magnetic lock closes itself when the upper panel goes down, which really saves time.

Kite SET_WK22-583S-1 backpack outside:

  • has orthopedic Ergo Back with soft breathable pads which repeat the natural curves of the back;
  • has soft breathable S-shaped shoulder straps which reduce the load on shoulders;
  • has a removable chest strap that fixes the straps in the comfortable position and distributes the weight properly;
  • has a removable waist strap that provides additional support and reduces the load;
  • has a convenient FIDLOCK magnetic lock which easily fastens in one movement;
  • has 2 side pockets with zippers for a water bottle or a thermos;
  • has 2 side pockets for small items;
  • has an ergonomic handle with plastic insert for carrying;
  • has a textile loop handle;
  • has a padded rubbered PU bottom with plastic legs - it is stable and easy to clean;
  • has light-reflecting elements from all sides to make the child visible on the road when it is dark outside;
  • has durable secure fittings such as smoothly working zippers and nice-touch pullers with rubber coating;
  • it is decorated with print, butterfly embroidery, a handmade bauble bracelet, a strap with a metal half-ring where a key chain can be attached, 3D Wonder Kite rubber badge.

Kite SET_WK22-583S-1 backpack inside:

  • has 1 spacious compartment;
  • has a large capacity front pocket with removable key chain inside;
  • has a special inner dividing system with elastic fixing band for books and notebooks;
  • has a tablet pocket with soft plush inside;
  • has a phone pocket with soft plush to protect the screen from scratches;
  • has inside pocket for small items;
  • has a transparent pocket with a paper insert for timetable;
  • has a special tag for personal information;
  • has icons telling the purpose of each pocket for an easy navigation.
  • has a durable branded lining.

2. Kite textile shoe bag is a convenient accessory for your second pair of shoes or clothes. It closes with a drawstring from both sides and turns into a mini backpack that can be carried on the back. It fits in your pocket when folded.

Durable wear-resistant polyester withstands the loads while the print is fade-resistant. This shoe bag for PE lesson is easy to wash with your hands, dries quickly and retains its original appearance for a long time.

Shoe bag characteristics:

  • 1 compartment;
  • 1 spacious front pocket with zipper;
  • a loop for a hanger;
  • drawstring closure;
  • is made of polyester;
  • size: 46x33cm;
  • is decorated with print, embroidery, 3D rubber badge with logo.

3. Kite hard-shaped pencil case will help your child to stay organized. During the lessons, kids not only write but also paint, cut and draw. This pencil case with big capacity and 2 flaps fits the entire set of basic stationary for every day.

Pencil case characteristics:

  • 1 compartment with 2 flaps to keep all the necessary items close at hand;
  • elastic strap holders for stationary - to keep the pencil case tidy;
  • fabric binding to protect the edges;
  • size: 19,5x13x3,7cm;
  • two transparent pockets with cardboard tabs (school schedule and multiplication table);
  • a tab with the multiplication table will help you to remember it better;
  • made of durable polyester from outside;
  • PVC material inside.
